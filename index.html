  • Chicken with Garlic sauce

    Main Ingredients

    1. 2 tablespoons peanut oil
    2. 6 cloves garlic, minced
    3. 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  • Shrimp and Rice

    Main Ingredients

    1. 1 1/2 c. long grain white rice (such as jasmine or basmati), rinsed until water runs clear
    2. 1 medium shallot, finely chopped
    3. 2 3/4 c. chicken stock

  • Blackened Salmon

    Main Ingredients

    1. 4 salmon fillets
    2. 1 teaspoon paprika
    3. 1 teaspoon black pepper to taste
